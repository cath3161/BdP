About us


Welcome to the homepage of Pfadfinderstamm Nydam from Kiel.
We don't know the word ¨bored¨ with the scouts, there is always somthing going on.
Our groups go around in the nature, engage themselves, thinker, play musik or do theater.
What kind of activities that we do are impulsiv,but still the themes and social role are playing an important role.
The idea for scouting came from Robert Baden-Powell, and we still live by his idea. for us that means,
getting to know everthing and everyone, where ever we go.
Doing what we have to for the community, group and city.
Know the interest of all members and try them out.
Make friendship across countries, religion or origin.
Not talk about political or religions themes to be open to everyone.
¨Leave the world better behind, than how we found it¨
We are happy to welcome new ¨Wolflings¨form the age of 7.
New scouts from the age of 12 and new rangers from age of 16.
Who want to help and support our wark.

Life as a scout


To live as a scouts meanes: Living in a group, learning togrther and making memories togrther.
Group doesn't mean: everyone is euqual. Everyone brings there own possibilities and knoleadge.
But no matter how diffrent scouts can be in a group, they all fell conneted.
To be a scout is an offer for all ages. To stimulate all ages, there are diffrent groups, with diffrent activities.
Tere are three diffrent groups, ¨Wolflings¨, scouts and rangers and rover.
The international recognition sign. All scouts wear the scarf proudly and thereby show
the whole world taht they belong to the biggest youth movement in the world.
Try out new things, dicover new things. Make a wager: We want to show kids and young people taht adventures belong to everyday life.
That you can't do everything at once, but that you can learn a lot.
We give the scouts time and space to figure out there own strengths.
The big adventure lures: The scouts are happy to go to the diffrent camps and they swim in the
memories from prior years of acamp.
Being away from home and everyday life, gives the scouts the possibelity to restrict everything
to what fits in there backpack.
To scout is being in nature and being conneted to the nature.
From the beginning life in nature was a way for us to discover the essenitial.

Scout ranks


The pack: The Wolflings meet in the pack. young scouts from the ages 6/7 to 11.
There are games to play and small adventures in the nature with other members.
At (Ratsfelsen) the wolflings meet up with the wise leaderwolf Akela, from the junglebook and together they talk
about the grouphours and themes. All the wolflings have a say there.

The clan: In one clan there are five to seven scout members, from the ages 11/12 to 15.
Together as a group they experiense exiting adventures and fun with their group.
While doing so, they learn to take resopnsibility for themselves and their group.
The planning, organizing and carrying out their own camps, tripes or other actions,
is part of the their homemade program. During that they learn responsibility,
get creative and can make their own decisions and stand by them.
A lot of properties and crafts, that can help them later on in life,is something they learn and grow to do.

The round: The ranger and rover (R/R) meet up in the round. It is young executives between
the ages 16 and 25, with responsibilityfor wolfling, scouts, administration jobs or just for fun,
because own actions with peers also belong to begin in (R/R). some of them take
jobs for the (Stammesführung oder Landesverband).

The adults: Also adults 25+ can have a place in the group.
Which fits the motto ¨youth leads youth¨, they can be in the backgrund and help the R/R and scouts with advice.
They know that own experience is worthsomething and they are not the fun keepers of the younger groups.
but their support and help by ¨do it yourself¨.
At the same time they give the young ones alot of space to make their own experience .
For the adults there are also (Altpfadfindergilden), adultactions of diffrent kinds
and the possibility to be at every (Stammesaktion).
